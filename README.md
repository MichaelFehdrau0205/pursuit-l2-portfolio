terponthego
PROBLEM:
Deaf individuals often struggle to find qualified ASL interpreters quickly when they need one most — for medical appointments, job interviews, legal situations, and everyday life. For millions of Deaf Americans, this process is slow, fragmented, and unreliable. TERP on the Go was built to fix that — a real-time booking platform that connects Deaf users with nearby interpreters based on location, availability, and need.


SOLUTION:
TERP on the Go is a full-stack booking platform that connects Deaf users with qualified ASL interpreters in real time using GPS-based matching. Built with React, Node.js, and PostgreSQL, it features dual dashboards, JWT authentication, and payment processing — giving both users and interpreters a seamless experience. Users can browse available interpreters nearby, book instantly, and manage appointments, while interpreters manage availability, accept requests, and get paid — making the whole process faster and more reliable for both sides.


LIST KEY FEATURES:
GPS-Based Interpreter Matching — Connects Deaf users with nearby available interpreters in real time based on location

Dual Dashboards — Separate, purpose-built interfaces for Deaf users and interpreters to manage bookings and availability

Instant Booking System — Users can search, select, and book an interpreter quickly without back-and-forth communication

JWT Authentication — Secure login and user verification for both Deaf users and interpreters

Integrated Payment Processing — Seamless in-app payments so interpreters get paid and users can book without leaving the platform

CSS (custom styling with dual color schemes — wine red for Deaf users, grass green for interpreters)

Backend>

REFLECT ON WHAT YOU LEARNED:
What I Learned-
Building TERP on the Go taught me how to architect a full-stack application from the ground up — connecting a React frontend to a Node.js backend with a real PostgreSQL database. I deepened my understanding of JWT authentication, GPS-based logic, and how to design two completely different user experiences within one platform.

What Was Challenging-
The biggest challenge was building dual dashboards that felt distinct but lived inside the same app. Managing different user roles, permissions, and flows required careful planning on both the frontend and backend. Integrating real-time location matching and payment processing also pushed me beyond my comfort zone in the best way.

What I'm Proud Of-
I'm proud that I built something that solves a real problem for my community — the Deaf community. This wasn't just a practice project. TERP on the Go represents a gap that actually exists, and I built a working solution for it. That means everything to me.

greeneyesnyc
PROBLEM:
NYC building owners are facing serious financial penalties under Local Law 97 — one of the most aggressive building emissions laws in the country — but most have no clear way to track, understand, or reduce their energy usage before fines hit. The problem isn't just technical, it's a lack of visibility and guidance at the right time. Green Eyes NYC was built to give building owners the clarity and tools they need to stay compliant and avoid costly penalties.

SOLUTION:
Green Eyes NYC is a building energy management platform designed specifically for NYC property owners navigating Local Law 97 compliance. The platform provides real-time energy monitoring, emissions tracking, and actionable recommendations so owners can make informed decisions before deadlines arrive. Instead of reacting to penalties, Green Eyes NYC puts building owners in control — turning compliance from a burden into a manageable, data-driven process.

LIST KEY FEATURES:
Emissions & Energy Dashboard — Real-time visibility into a building's energy usage and emissions data in one centralized view
Local Law 97 Compliance Tracker — Monitors where a building stands against LL97 thresholds and upcoming penalty deadlines

Penalty Calculator — Estimates potential fines so owners understand the financial stakes before it's too late

Actionable Recommendations — Suggests specific steps building owners can take to reduce emissions and stay compliant

Building Portfolio Management — Allows owners and managers to track multiple properties from a single dashboard

TECH STACK:
React (Frontend)
Pair: Elliot Chan > Backend - Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, Database
Pair: Joel Philip > Integration & Docs

What I Learned-

What Was Challenging-

What I'm Proud Of-
